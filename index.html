About Me

Hello! My name is Gabriella Valencia Trujillo-Robnett (say that three times fast) and I grew up in the Bay Area- East Side San Jose to be specific. I moved to Colorado six years ago and while I don't ski or snowboard, I do make the grueling and arduous trek to the local dog park to play with my needy German Sheperds, Kenai and Kovu. I moved here to focus on my passions, which are singing, acting and dancing- next to coding of course. My coding journey started when I was a mere padawan, teaching myself basic HTML and CSS while playing Neopets. I couldn't wait to get home and edit background images and font colors for hours, and not much has changed since then.
